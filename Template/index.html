Society of Civil and Environmental Engineering

Society of Civil and Environmental Engineering,
DTU


Society for Civil and Environmental Engineers (SCEE) is a society of Delhi Technological University (formerly Delhi College of Engineering) which works towards providing practical and innovative paths of learning in the field of Civil and Environmental engineering. The society was founded in the year 2009. The society works to provide its members various opportunities such as to participate in events related to bestowing practical knowledge in the field of civil and environmental engineering. The society also aims at providing various workshops, seminars, Student Interest Groups (SIGs), projects and field visits.

The society is under our faculty advisor Prof. A.K. Srivastava and guided by the H.O.D of Civil Engineering Department Prof. Nirendra Dev. The society works towards providing vital practical knowledge and learning to all the members and students.

Geocells

For engineers, it has been a routine challenge how to optimally manage available natural and capital to construct high-quality roads and to minimize repair and maintenance costs, the poor tensile strength of the soil. A sustainable option to manage high quality is to stabilize pavement structures by using some stabilization techniques that improve pavement structural strength, lessen repair and maintenance costs, and use on-site or recycled materials and we also need the soil to be strengthened, which is done by strengthening the soil mass is to improve stability, increase bearing capacity and reduce settlements and lateral deformation. There are several methods to approach the other problem, one of those methods is geocell reinforcement.
